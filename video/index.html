The video program will be made accessible from this site that will also be used to centralize the recorded processes of the research exercise. It is integral that access to this component of the project is uninhibited. However, similar to the research project, various screenings shall be held of the program in a variety of arts, pedagogical, or domestic venues. The program is not intended to be a finished list of works. The program will be continuously updated as new works are added.
